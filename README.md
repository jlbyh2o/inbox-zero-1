## About Inbox Zero

Inbox Zero is an open-source AI email assistant that helps you:
- Organize your inbox automatically
- Pre-draft replies in your tone and style
- Track follow-ups and emails awaiting responses
- Bulk unsubscribe from newsletters
- Block cold emails
- Analyze your email activity
